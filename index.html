The Embroidery process

Here we start with the process.

The Art work

An Artist creates the design on a tracing paper.This decides the placement of embroidery on fabic.

Pinning

This tool makes tiny holes along the design.

Framing

The base fabric is stiched and streched on the frame.

Design Transfer

The pinned artwork design is placed on the base fabric and a thin paste of print is rubbed over it.The paste passes through the tiny holes & now we have the designed transfered onto fabric.

Beading

And then begins the process of beading & you can see the design into life.
